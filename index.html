How to sharpen pocket knife with stone?

If you are an outdoor person or you are an active person, you must have come across the situation where you have lost your pocket knife, it is really annoying to lose your pocket knife. In most of the cases, they get damaged because they are not sharpened properly. But, don’t worry if you have a pocket knife with you then you can sharpen it using a stone. There are many methods of sharpening a knife with stone but today I will share one method with you which is very simple and effective. So, let’s start.

Materials required:

  1. Knife
  2. Stone
  3. Wood
  4. Knife Sharpener

Steps to sharpen pocket knife with stone

  1. Take out the knife from its sheath.
  2. Hold the knife in your hand and remove all the dirt from it.
  3. Place the stone on the knife and then press it with the wood.
  4. Turn the knife over and apply pressure for few seconds.
  5. After that, turn the knife back and repeat the same process.
